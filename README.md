# Privacy Policy

**GSG Talent Platform**

Effective Date: 12/3/2026 · Last Updated: 12/3/2026

---

This Privacy Policy describes how Gaza Sky Geeks ("GSG"), a program of Mercy Corps, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the GSG Talent Platform (the "Platform"). The Platform is operated by GSG and is intended for Palestinian community members aged 18 and older.

By creating an account on the Platform, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described below. If you do not agree, please do not create an account.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Gaza Sky Geeks (GSG) is a Palestinian technology and entrepreneurship accelerator and a program of Mercy Corps. We operate the Talent Platform to connect our community members with employment opportunities, employer partners, and career support services. For the purposes of this policy, GSG, operating under Mercy Corps, is the data controller responsible for your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information you provide.** When you create and update your profile, you may provide:

- Name, email address, phone number, and location
- Date of birth and gender
- Education history (university, degree, graduation year)
- Work experience, internships, and employment status
- Technical skills, tools, and proficiency levels
- Portfolio links (GitHub, personal website, project samples)
- Career preferences (desired role, availability, remote/on-site, target industry)
- CV, resume, or other documents you upload

**Information collected automatically.** When you use the Platform, we may automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate location
- Browser type, device type, and operating system
- Pages visited and features used
- Login times and session duration

**Information from other sources.** If you import your profile from LinkedIn or upload a CV, we may receive information from those sources as authorized by you.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Match you with job opportunities and refer you to employer partners
- Provide career support services (coaching, CV review, interview preparation)
- Connect your training history to your employability profile
- Send you profile update reminders and opportunity notifications
- Improve the Platform's features and usability using aggregate, anonymized data
- Analyze user behavior and platform usage patterns through third-party analytics tools to enhance the platform experience
- Produce anonymized reports on community skills and employment outcomes for internal planning and donor reporting

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following ways:

**With employer partners.** If your profile is set to "visible," we may share it with employer partners when you are a potential match for a role. You control your visibility settings and can set your profile to private at any time.

**With service providers.** We use third-party providers (hosting, email, analytics) to operate the Platform. These providers access your data only as needed to perform their services and are required to protect it.

**With analytics providers.** We use third-party analytics tools to track user behavior and platform usage patterns. This data helps us understand how the Platform is used and improve the experience. Analytics providers may collect information such as pages visited, features used, session duration, and device information. Where possible, this data is collected in anonymized or pseudonymized form.

**With GSG staff.** Your profile is accessible to GSG team members involved in employability services and platform management, on a need-to-know basis.

**In anonymized form.** We may share aggregate, anonymized data that does not identify you individually with donors, partners, and the public for impact reporting.

**When required by law.** We may disclose your information if required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access and correction.** You can view and edit your profile information at any time by logging into the Platform.
- **Visibility control.** You can set your profile to "visible" (discoverable by employers) or "private" (hidden from employer searches) at any time.
- **Data export.** You may request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting the Employability & Tech Unit.
- **Deletion.** You may request that your account and all associated data be deleted. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Deleting your account removes you from all employability services.
- **Withdraw consent.** You may withdraw consent for employer sharing at any time by setting your profile to private. Withdrawal does not affect sharing that occurred before you changed your settings.
- **Communication preferences.** You can opt out of non-essential notifications (such as opportunity alerts) while still receiving required Platform communications (such as profile update reminders).

To exercise any of these rights, contact the Employability & Tech Unit at work@gazaskygeeks.com.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If your profile is inactive for more than 180 days, it may be archived and removed from active employer searches. You can reactivate it at any time by logging in and updating your information.

If you request account deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records by law or for anonymized reporting purposes.

Anonymized, aggregate data that does not identify you may be retained indefinitely for research and reporting.

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

- Encrypting data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Restricting access to authorized GSG staff based on role
- Storing data on secure, industry-standard hosting infrastructure
- Conducting regular reviews of access controls and security practices

No system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, please contact us immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

GSG operates in Palestine as a program of Mercy Corps. Some of our service providers, analytics tools, and employer partners may be located outside Palestine. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in Palestine. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is protected regardless of where it is processed.

## 9. Age Requirement

The Platform is intended for Palestinian community members aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via in-platform notification and email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The revised policy will include an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Your continued use of the Platform after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
